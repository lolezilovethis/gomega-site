School Games — Official Notice & Terms

Last updated: August 21, 2025

Important summary:
  • https://gomega.watch/schoolgames was originally intended to host casual school games, but it is no longer active and will not be restored.
  • The School Games page will never be up again in its prior form.
  • These terms are a formal notice and will be added to our official site when we have time to publish them site-wide.

Background

The School Games area was an experimental section intended for casual gaming in safe, supervised environments. Over time we determined that hosting that content publicly created misuse and conflicts with school policies. For this reason, the section has been retired and will not be re-launched.

Message to Students & Users

This service is not intended to help students bypass school network rules, classroom management, or filtering. If you are a student or a user within a school environment, you should follow your institution's acceptable use policies. We are not responsible for any disciplinary or other consequences that result from using Gomega contrary to your school’s rules.

Discipline & Enforcement: Accounts that misuse the Service, break our Terms of Service, or disrupt other users may be suspended or terminated at our discretion. We reserve the right to ban accounts for cause or for no reason, consistent with our Terms.

Message to School Administrators

We take legitimate administrative concerns seriously. If your school has detected students using our Service in violation of school policy, we are willing to cooperate to the extent possible and lawful.

To help, we can:

  • Review available logs and account activity associated with your report.
  • Provide account-related information or take action against accounts when presented with a valid, documented request or lawful process.
  • Discuss technical or administrative steps schools can use to limit access (e.g., network-level blocking, using device management tools, or contacting us for assistance).

How to contact us: Send a detailed request to support@gomega.watch including timestamps, IP addresses (if available), usernames, and any relevant screenshots. We will respond and cooperate consistent with applicable law and our privacy obligations.
