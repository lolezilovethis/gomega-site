This is the end of Gomega

A last quiet hour from the people who kept it alive

There are empty chairs in chat rooms and fragile echoes down the threads — places that used to hum now hold a softer sound, the sort you hear when a room waits for footsteps that will not return. We built this small place from scraps of curiosity and late-night stubbornness; we shared scripts, silly failures, solutions that helped someone at 2 a.m., and the kind of kindness that does not ask for anything in return.

The owner can no longer keep the lights on: hosting, domain, backups — it all costs more than our pockets can cover. We've stretched and trimmed and held small fund drives, but there is an inevitable hush until an unexpected kindness saves one more month. This page is both a farewell and an invitation to gather your pieces — to save pages, screenshots, and the tiny memories stitched across profiles and comments.

If Gomega ever mattered to you — if you laughed here, learned here, or found a friend between lines of code — please keep that memory close. If you can mirror a page or save a thread, you will be doing a quiet, enormous thing. If you cannot, hold the memory anyway. Some endings are gentle; some are sharp — ours is a slow, honest dimming.

We close with gratitude and a bruise of sorrow. Thank you for the bugs you reported, the late-night fixes, the shared scripts, the jokes in commit messages, and the people who showed up when they could. ENDING MAR 7 2026

All content will remain accessible while we can — please archive what you want to keep.
— The Team
